This is: Sophisticated Apes

Welcome to the fan-made band page for Sophisticated Apes, a musical duo from Virginia Beach, VA. Consisting of the artists DT and KP, Sophisticated Apes is a genre-defying act who graces many a venue in the duo's hometown of Virginia Beach. DT and KP have known each other since they were wee little lads, and they started playing music together in middle school. Now in their mid-twenties, the group often covers bands like the Grateful Dead Steely Dan, while also throwing in some of their own stuff from time to time.

Since 2019 the duo has been collaborating on their musical endeavors over the phone, as KP got a gig in the Peace Corps and is out of the country for the time being. But don't despair, the group is still hard at work and might just be putting out an album one of these days. heck back for future tour dates or album announcements! Until then, feel free to check out past performances and recordings linked above.

Thanks for taking the time to check us out, and happy listening!
